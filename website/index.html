How It Works
From Order to Verified Trade in Minutes

A streamlined workflow that handles the complexity of water rights trading behind a simple interface.

1

Create Your Account

Register with your farm details, basin, GSA affiliation, and water allocation. Your balance is tracked in acre-feet.

2

Place an Order

Submit a buy or sell order specifying quantity (AF) and your price per acre-foot. View the order book for current market conditions.

3

Automated Matching

The matching engine finds compatible orders using price-time priority, then scores them for economic and environmental optimality.

4

Compliance Verified

Each match is run through 12 SGMA compliance checks using real GSP data, knowledge graph traversal, and AI reasoning.

SGMA Compliance
Built on Real Regulatory Data

Every compliance decision is grounded in actual Kern County GSP 2025 numbers and SGMA statute text — not assumptions.

  • ✓
    Knowledge graph with two-tree architecture — Legal/Governance and Physical/Hydrology — connected by bridge entities
  • ✓
    Policy engine indexes real GSP text chunks with page-level citations from Kern County GSP 2025
  • ✓
    12 compliance questions covering transfer legality, sustainability, subsidence, water quality, well interference, and drought conditions
  • ✓
    RAG pipeline retrieves relevant policy context before LLM evaluates each question
  • ✓
    Final verdict: APPROVED, CONDITIONALLY APPROVED, or DENIED — with conditions and monitoring requirements
